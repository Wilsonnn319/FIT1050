About us

who we are and what we do

TimeWeb is located in South Melbourne, Victoria. If you are a local business or resident, we can come to you and provide all your hardware requirements. We endeavour to provide high quality customer service and after sales support. TimeWeb has been in operation for over 10 years, and have a strong focus on customer support and understanding our clients needs.

Shipping Info

TimeWeb offers both local pickup as well as delivery through Australia Post. Unlike many other online computer stores, we do have a storefront and offer great quality after sales service. We have a high rate of repeat customers, and strongly believe that this is a result of our high customer focus.
